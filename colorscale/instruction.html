Everything about Hex Sudoku: Classic

The Classic 4x4

It is the easiest to play and fastest since it contain the least logic to follow.

The Classic 6x6

It is the moderate of the three levels, and it gives a small challenge to the player.

The Classic 9x9

It is the hardest, the one that people choose to challenge themselves.

Everything about Hex Sudoku: Killer

The Killer 4x4

It is the easiest to play and fastest since it contain the least logic to follow and with addition blocks.

The Killer 6x6

It is the moderate of the three levels, and it gives a small challenge and with addition blocks to the player.

The Killer 9x9

It is the hardest, the one that people choose to challenge themselves. It also has some addition blocks.
